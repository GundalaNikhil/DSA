Scenario: Infrastructure network - find roads that are critical (single points of failure)!

Tarjan's Bridges Algorithm:

  • disc[u]: Discovery time of node u
  • low[u]: Lowest disc reachable from subtree of u
  • Bridge: Edge (u,v) is a bridge if low[v] > disc[u]

A bridge exists when there's no back edge to reach an ancestor!
