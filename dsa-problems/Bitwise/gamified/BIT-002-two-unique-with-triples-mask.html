BINARY SPLITTER

BIT-002
BIT SCANNER
Select a bit where count % 3 == 1
ZERO
ONE

PROTOCOL: [SPLIT]

Problem: Two Unique Agents are hidden among groups of identical Clones (3 copies each).

Mission: Isolate the two Agents.

Method:
1. Use the Bit Scanner to find a "Splitting Bit".
2. A Splitting Bit is one where the total count of set bits is 1 mod 3.
3. Select the bit to Divide the group into two chambers.
4. Purge the clones to reveal the Agents.
