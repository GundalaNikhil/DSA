MISSION BRIEFING

1. Objective: Identification of the rogue signal (unique number).

2. Intel: All signals appear in even pairs, except one. However, all signals are encrypted (Salted).

3. Tool: The XOR CORE. Dragging a signal into the core XORs it with the current value.

4. Strategy:
  a. Feed ALL standard signals into the Core. Pairs will cancel out `(A^A=0)`.
  b. The Core will surely hold the Encrypted Rogue.
  c. Finally, feed the SALT (Pink Chip) to decrypt the value.
