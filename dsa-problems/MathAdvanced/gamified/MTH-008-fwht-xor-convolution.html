What is XOR Convolution?

Instead of adding indices (normal convolution), we XOR them!

C[k] = Σ A[i] × B[j] where i ⊕ j = k

Walsh-Hadamard Transform:

Like FFT but for XOR operations. Works on arrays of size 2^k.

Steps:

1. Transform both A and B to WHT domain

2. Multiply pointwise

3. Inverse transform

Time: O(n log n) — much faster than O(n²)!
