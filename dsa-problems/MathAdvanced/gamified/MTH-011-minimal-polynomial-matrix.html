What is the Minimal Polynomial?

The smallest-degree monic polynomial p(x) such that p(A) = 0 (the zero matrix).

Krylov Method:

v, Av, A²v, A³v, ...

This sequence eventually becomes linearly dependent. The coefficients form the minimal polynomial!

Why it matters:

Knowing the minimal polynomial tells us about eigenvalues and helps solve matrix equations efficiently.
