PRIORITY BOARDING

GATE A-14
ECO (Front via Left)
VIP (Fixed)
BIZ (Rear via Right)

PASSENGER MANIFEST

1. Task: Sort the passengers based on class.
2. Order: ECO (0) -> VIP (1) -> BIZ (2).
3. Critical Rule: VIPs (1) are LOCKED in their seats. You cannot move them.
4. Action: Drag and Drop ECO/BIZ passengers to valid empty slots (swapping them).
5. Hint: Move 0s to available slots on Left, 2s to available slots on Right.
